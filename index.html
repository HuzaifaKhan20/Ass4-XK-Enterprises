About Us

XK Enterprises, a Business Entity working in the field of seafood industry since 1971 and having successful 45 years by providing Fresh, Frozen, and Live quality seafood. The factory is situated in the vicinity of Fishing Port and Central Fish Market of Karachi. The residing sea to Karachi is the Arabian Sea from where our focal seafood products are netted. Nowadays, XK Enterprises are being successfully handled by the third generation of the Khan Family, Mr. Xain Khan. M/s. XK Enterprises is approved by the Marine Fisheries Department, which is the Government body to control the Seafood Industry. We are also the Member of The Karachi Chamber of Commerce & Industry & Pakistan Fisheries Exporters Association. The Factory has seafood processing area of 1600 sq. meters, a quick freezing capacity of 80-100 tons on a daily basis and storage facility of 600 Tons.
